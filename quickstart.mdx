## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you have a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. No special software or accounts are required beyond an email address for sign-up. An internet connection gets you started immediately.

<Callout kind="info">
Nexapaw works best on desktop or mobile browsers. Enable JavaScript and cookies for the smoothest shopping experience.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account or Sign In

Start by visiting [nexapaw.com](https://nexapaw.com). You can create a new account or sign in if you already have one.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New Account" icon="user-plus">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit Sign-Up Page" icon="external-link">
        Click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
        Provide your email, create a strong password, and add your name.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
        Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Existing Account" icon="log-in">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click Sign In" icon="log-in">
        Select **Sign In** from the top navigation.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Credentials" icon="key">
        Input your registered email and password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete Login" icon="check-circle">
        Click **Sign In**. Use **Forgot Password?** if needed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Browse and Search for Accessories

Once logged in, explore Nexapaw's collection of Biothane leashes, collars, harnesses, and smart ID tags.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate Categories" icon="search">
    Use the main menu: hover over **Leashes**, **Collars**, or **Harnesses** to see options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Products" icon="zoom-in">
    Enter terms like `waterproof leash` or `small dog harness` in the search bar at the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Results" icon="filter">
    Refine by size, color, or material using the sidebar filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Biothane products are odor-free and waterproof. Look for the **Best Seller** badge for popular items.
</Callout>

## Add to Cart and Checkout

Ready to buy? Add items and complete your purchase securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Product" icon="shopping-cart">
    Click a product image, choose size/color, then **Add to Cart**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Cart" icon="package">
    Click the cart icon (top-right) to view items. Adjust quantities or remove as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proceed to Checkout" icon="credit-card">
    Enter shipping address, select payment method (credit card, PayPal), and review order summary.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Order" icon="check">
    Confirm and place your order. You'll receive an email confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
