## Overview

Access your Nexapaw account to update personal details, track orders, manage payments, and handle returns. Log in at `https://nexapaw.com/account` using your email and password. The dashboard provides a centralized view of your shopping activity.

<Callout kind="info">
  Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings for added security.
</Callout>

## Updating Profile Information and Addresses

Navigate to the Profile section in your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile" icon="user">
    Click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select `Profile Settings`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Details">
    Update your name, phone number, or email. Verify changes with a confirmation code sent to your new email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Addresses">
    Add a new shipping address or set a default one. Include details like:
    
    | Field       | Example                  |
    |-------------|--------------------------|
    | Full Name   | John Doe                 |
    | Street      | 123 Bark Lane            |
    | City        | Seattle, WA 98101        |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click `Save Profile`. Changes apply immediately to future orders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Keep your addresses updated to avoid shipping delays on new leashes or collars.
</Callout>

## Managing Saved Payment Methods

<Expandable title="Adding a New Payment Method" default-open="true">
  1. Go to `Payments` in your dashboard.
  2. Click `Add Card`.
  3. Enter card details securely (we use tokenization for safety).
  4. Set as default if desired.
</Expandable>

To remove a method:
- Select the card.
- Click `Remove`.
- Confirm the action.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Never share your payment details. Nexapaw stores only encrypted tokens.
</Callout>

## Handling Returns and Customer Support

For returns within 30 days:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Return" icon="arrow-left">
    Open the order and click `Request Return`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Items">
    Choose products like the harness or collar, specify reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Print Label">
    Generate and print the prepaid label.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ship & Track">
    Drop off at the carrier. Track via your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For support, use the `Help` section to submit tickets or chat live. Response time is typically `<24 hours`.

<Expandable title="Common Support Topics">
  - Order issues
  - Product sizing for breeds
  - Biothane care instructions
</Expandable>
