---
title: Smart ID Tag Not Working
description: Fix issues with your Nexapaw Smart ID tag.
---

## Symptoms

- Tag does not scan
- No owner info appears

## Causes

- Dirty QR code
- App not updated

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clean the tag">
    ````
    Wipe the QR code gently with a damp cloth.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update app">
    ````
    Ensure your scanner app is updated to the latest version.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
