## Introduction

Henadzi Yermachonak is the founder of Nexapaw, an online retailer specializing in durable dog accessories.

## Background

Henadzi started Nexapaw to give fellow dog owners access to quality, stylish leashes, collars, and harnesses using Biothane material.

## Nexapaw Mission

Henadzi's focus is combining practical features with fashionable design, aiming for comfortable, waterproof, and easy-to-care-for pet gear.
